Refund & Rescheduling Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Booking Confirmation

Your consultation slot is only confirmed upon receipt of full payment via UPI and verification of the transaction receipt sent to our WhatsApp or Email.

2. Refunds

Due to the time required to manually calculate, cast, and study your charts prior to the live reading, we maintain a strict no-refund policy once the Google Meet link has been sent.

If you request a cancellation before we have generated and sent the meeting link (usually within 12 hours of payment), a full refund will be initiated to your original payment method.

3. Rescheduling

We understand that life happens. If you need to reschedule your reading, please notify us at least 24 hours before your confirmed time slot via WhatsApp or Email.

  • You may reschedule your appointment once within a 30-day window.
  • No-shows at the scheduled Google Meet time will not be eligible for a refund or a free reschedule. The time is reserved exclusively for you.
  • If you arrive more than 15 minutes late to a 45-minute or 60-minute session, the session will be considered a no-show.

4. Contact Us

For any queries related to refunds or rescheduling, please reach out to: sarthakbhattacharyyya@gmail.com
