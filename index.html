My Ishta Devta 🦚🏹

Lord Kārtikeya, also known as Skanda, Murugan, and Mahāsenā, is the eternal commander of the celestial army. Born from the sparks of Lord Shiva's third eye, he represents the supreme manifestation of consciousness and the ultimate victory of wisdom over ignorance.

In the spiritual evolution of the soul, Kārtikeya symbolizes the Vel (Divine Spear)—the razor-sharp intellect that pierces through the illusions of the material world. He is the guardian of the Krittika nakshatra energy, teaching us to focus our internal heat (Tapas) into a single-pointed direction to slay the demons of ego and vanity.

Riding upon a peacock, which represents the human ego and fickle mind conquered, he leads the soul from the battlefield of confusion to the heights of spiritual clarity. For me, he is the source of strength, discipline, and the unwavering light of the inner sun.
