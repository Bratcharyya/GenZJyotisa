Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you book a consultation with GenZ Jyotiṣa, we collect the following personal information to accurately generate and analyze your birth chart:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • WhatsApp Number
  • Birth Details (Date, Exact Time, and Place of Birth)
  • Specific questions or areas of focus you provide

2. How We Use Your Information

Your birth details and personal information are used strictly for the purpose of the astrological consultation. We use this data to:

  • Cast your Vedic astrological charts (Rāśi, Navāṁśa, etc.).
  • Communicate with you regarding your booking, payment, and meeting link.
  • Provide personalized insights during your session.

3. Confidentiality and Data Sharing

Astrological consultations are deeply personal. We treat your data with the utmost confidentiality. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or birth data with any third parties under any circumstances. Notes taken during your session are kept private and secure.

4. Payment Information

We do not collect or store credit card details or sensitive banking information. All payments are processed manually via secure UPI transactions between you and your bank.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your birth data and related communications from our records after your consultation is complete. To do so, please contact us at sarthakbhattacharyyya@gmail.com.
